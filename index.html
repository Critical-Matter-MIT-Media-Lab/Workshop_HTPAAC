Key Principles:

• Left to right signal flow
• Top to bottom power flow  
• Group related components
• Clear net labels
• Consistent symbol usage

Clean, readable schematics save debugging time

Layout Design Tips

Critical Considerations

  • ⚡ Keep high-speed signals short
  • 🌊 Avoid 90° angles on traces
  • 🔌 Separate analog and digital grounds
  • 🎯 Place decoupling caps close to ICs
  • 🔥 Consider thermal management

Component Selection

  • 📊 Check electrical specifications
  • 💰 Consider cost vs. performance
  • 🚚 Verify availability and lead times
  • 📎 Package size and footprint
  • 🌡️ Temperature and environmental ratings

Signal Integrity

High-Speed Design Rules:

• Match trace lengths for differential pairs
• Control impedance (50Ω, 100Ω diff)
• Minimize via count on critical signals
• Guard traces for sensitive signals
• Consider crosstalk between traces

Critical for high-frequency and digital circuits
